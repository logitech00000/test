Features

Why should you join
Protothon

Build and Learn Faster

Protothon forces you to move from idea to a working prototype in just 24 hours. This intensive, hands-on pressure is the fastest way to master new programming and hardware skills that truly matter.

Boost Your Career Portfolio

Your finished project becomes a tangible, powerful addition to your portfolio and resume. Show employers your ability to execute, problem-solve, and deliver under a tight, real-world deadline.

Network with Experts

Work alongside and learn directly from industry professionals and ECE faculty mentors. You'll strengthen your teamwork and communication skills while building valuable connections that last beyond the event.

Expert Mentorship & Feedback

You'll have access to experienced mentors and subject matter experts from the Department and sponsoring companies offering invaluable career insights.

Unleash Innovation & Creativity

Protothon provide a risk-free environment to explore "out-of-the-box" solutions to challenging problems and turn abstract ideas into functional realities.

Prizes & Networking

Finish strong to claim cash, tech gear, and unique prizes. More importantly,It’s the ultimate opportunity to network and showcase your skills for potential internships or job offers.
